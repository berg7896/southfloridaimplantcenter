Common Questions
Dental Implants FAQ

How much does a single dental implant cost?

A single dental implant at South Florida Implant Center is just $1,789. This includes the implant, abutment, and custom crown — plus a 5-year warranty with our in-house discount plan.

Is getting a dental implant painful?

Most patients report the procedure is much easier than expected. We use local anesthesia and sedation options to ensure you're completely comfortable. Post-procedure discomfort is typically managed with over-the-counter pain medication.

How long does the implant process take?

The implant is placed in a single visit (about 1 hour). After 3-4 months of healing, your permanent crown is attached in a second visit. You'll have a temporary restoration in the meantime.

Am I a candidate for dental implants?

Most adults with missing teeth are candidates. A free consultation with 3D CT scan is the best way to find out. Even patients with some bone loss may qualify.
