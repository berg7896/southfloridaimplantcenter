Common Questions
All-on-4 FAQ

How much do All-on-4 dental implants cost?

All-on-4 costs typically range from $20,000 to $40,000 per arch. During your free consultation, we'll provide an exact quote and walk you through financing options that can bring your monthly payment to as low as $299/month.

Is the All-on-4 procedure painful?

Most patients are surprised by how comfortable it is. We use IV sedation so you'll be relaxed and pain-free throughout. Most patients return to normal activities within 2-3 days.

How long does the procedure take?

The surgical procedure takes approximately 2-3 hours per arch. You'll leave the same day with a full set of temporary teeth. Your permanent teeth are placed after 3-6 months of healing.

Am I a candidate for All-on-4?

Most adults with missing, failing, or severely damaged teeth are candidates. The procedure works with minimal bone density, so even patients told they "don't have enough bone" may qualify. A free consultation with 3D CT scan is the best way to find out.
